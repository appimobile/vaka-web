Welcome to Vaka Academy!

Vaka Academy was established late October 2016. Vaka Academy was born out of the passion of wanting to see others learn and understand the core skills when it comes to development that others might think to seem is so difficult. The founding members of Vaka Academy both run their own individual businesses and teamed up to form this academy that teaches people core skills to improve their livelihood.

Having over 10years of industry experience, we are Vaka Academy have identified certain skills that seem to be a regular request in the business market place, this is the case with the mobile applications development course. Vaka Avademy was built from this and then we later on decided to add additional courses to the list. Planned courses include:

  • 2 Mobile Applications Prototyping
  • Native Android Development
  • Native iOS Development
  • Hybrid Development for Android and iOS
  • Apple TV Development
  • Video Production - Editing
  • Designing for Web and Mobile Apps using Adobe CC
